

## Plan: "Meet Your AI Team" вҖ” Landing Page Agent Showcase

### The Idea

Add a new dedicated section to the landing page between FeatureGrid and SocialProof called **"Meet Your AI Team"**. Instead of abstract feature descriptions, visitors see actual AI agent personas вҖ” each with a face/avatar, name, a one-liner about what they do, and which part of the platform they live in. This makes the AI feel human, approachable, and embedded rather than bolted on.

Each card:
- Large round avatar area with the agent's emoji avatar (from DB seed) or a generated initial
- A pulsing green "active" dot (reusing `AIIndicator`)
- Agent name in bold
- A first-person quote: "Hi, I'm [name]. I [whatIDo]." вҖ” written conversationally
- A subtle "рҹ“Қ Lives in: [section]" tag showing where in the app they work
- Hover: card lifts with `shadow-ai`, border glows `border-primary/30`

- On mobile (`< lg`): horizontal scroll carousel with snap points, 2 cards visible at a time
